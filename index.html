Who are the fatherless?

... Our site is currently undergoing updating to a more responsive format to allow viewing on a cell phone and a tablet. Thank you for your patience.

Today we find fatherless people in many different places. They can be forsaken children who have been dropped off at an orphanage to be raised in a difficult environment. They can be homeless people we encounter on the street looking for their next meal or their next drink or high? They can be widows who have been neglected by family and are facing difficult choices choosing between purchasing needed medicine or buying food so they can eat. Today like no time in our history is there a real need to reach out and pierce the darkness and offer a way out of this madness.

Since 2009, Endless Hope Foundation has been working in Europe reaching out and helping neglected orphans, homeless, widows, and others. We have groups who go out onto the streets and serve meals to the homeless and offer them a way out of their darkness. We support rehabilitation centers who provide a safe stable environment for them to get off alcohol and drugs. We go and visit orphans in government run institutuions and provide things that are not usually provided for them. We also share the good news of the gospel of Jesus Christ.
